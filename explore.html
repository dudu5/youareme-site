My Perspectives

This is where I share my perspectives... Read more ↓

This is where I share my perspectives. They are just that — my perspectives. They have been forged through my life experience, everything I have listened to, read, feelt and thought of, interacted with. I'm not claiming they are right or true, they are just what it seems like to me.

My experience has taught me that I have everything to win by articulating what I see and understand as clearly as I can — both for my own clarity, but also to enable more qualitative alignment with others.

Another thing I've learned is that my perspectives are dynamic, constantly evolving. The biggest treasure in my self development has been learning to not defend but be curious about different perspectives — especially when my body wants to defend. The more I treat my perspectives as fixed truths that define me, the more trapped I become by them. When I can hold them lightly, as working hypotheses rather than identity, I stay free to grow.

I invite you to engage with these perspectives as starting points for your own exploration. What resonates, what challenges, what sparks curiosity about your own experience? How do you navigate the experience of being you?
