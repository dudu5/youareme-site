Chapter 01
The Absolute Center

Where these maps come from

These maps didn't come from research.

They came from living — through failure, through fear, through years of trying to understand what moves human beings, and how to navigate being one myself.

Thousands of hours of listening, reading, thinking, mapping. Countless iterations — maps built and rebuilt, patterns recognized and refined, structures synthesized and tested. In business. In workshops, coaching sessions, and circles.

Showing the maps to others — and facing the moment when they didn't see what I saw. I learned early that this always comes back to me: either I'm not communicating it clearly enough, or what I think I see isn't as solid as I believe. Both mean the same thing — go back. Look again. Rebuild.

That loop, repeated over years, is what these maps are made of. It taught me patience — how to really listen. First to others, then finally to myself.

They're not finished. They never will be. Each conversation refines them.

Which means by engaging now — you're not just working on yourself. You're helping build something that will serve everyone who comes after you.

The maps are still being released.
